About

This site serves to view results from an acetylation study.


Data


All samples are human platelets with multiple biological replicates, which means all the sample preparation and mass spectrometry are independent.


Working with the results


Isolated the peptides that apply for the following

  1. are modified by Acetyl
  2. choose the ones with the highest score
  3. choose the ones with rank 1
  4. calculate rank product using peptide score to sort them by significance
  5. filter by identification probability (pep_expect), where cutoff values are 0.05, 0.5 and 10
